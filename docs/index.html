Baby Word Tracker

Spring 2025 Capstone Project

The Baby Word Tracker is a tool to gain more detailed data on child speech development to assist in research at The University of Alabama's Literacy Education & Cognitive Science Lab. Baby Word Tracker is a mobile app that allows parents to track their child's speech development by inputing text, audio, and video data of their child's speech. Simple data analysis and visulization is available to parents while all data input into the app is avaibale to the LECSLab. Eventually, we hope that the app will be able to parse children's speech so that parents do not have to annotate audio and video input.
